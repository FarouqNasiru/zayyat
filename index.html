Our History

Zayyat Islamic Schools stands as a beacon of excellence in Islamic and academic education, committed to nurturing future leaders grounded in knowledge, discipline, and faith. Established to provide a holistic learning experience, the school integrates Islamic teachings with modern educational practices, ensuring students are well-equipped to excel in all facets of life.
Zayyat Islamic Schools was established on July 11, 2020, and is located at 32 Janet Akitola Street, Marshy Hill Estate, Akins, Addo Road, Ajah, Eti Osa Local Government Area, Lagos State. The school began its journey with a modest group of pioneer students: Lubabah Ahmad, Faaiq Nasiru, Mutmainna Abdulfattah Zakariyyah, Muhammad Olugobi, Sahnun Ahmad, Julaibib, and Shaimah Ahmad. Since then, it has grown to become a center of learning that integrates Islamic and secular education, preparing students for success in this world and the hereafter. The School has at the moment One Hundred and Twenty Nine (129) Students.
The school offers a unique blend of the Saudi Arabian and Lagos State curricula, catering to students at Preschool, Primary, and Secondary levels. Zayyat Islamic Schools is committed to providing a balanced education through programs such as Quran memorization, Tajweed, and Arabic Literature, alongside conventional subjects. This comprehensive approach ensures that students are equipped with both academic excellence and strong moral values.

Founder/Principal

Amb. AHMAD MANIR, FICMC, SIGPCM, CGArb, ACArb. Lagos State University Mathematics Education Usmanu Danfodiyo University, LL.B Common and Islamic Law B.L , Nigerian Law School Proprietor/ Principal Quran Memorization, Government and Civic Education.
Zayyat Islamic Schools was founded by Amb. Ahmad Manir, a distinguished lawyer and educator with a wealth of experience and a passion for knowledge dissemination. His academic and professional achievements reflect his commitment to education, law, and conflict resolution. Amb. Ahmad Manir began his academic journey with a first degree in Mathematics Education from Lagos State University (LASU). He furthered his studies in law, earning a degree in Common and Islamic Law from Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto, followed by a B.L from the Nigerian Law School, Lagos. He is a registered educator with the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria and has demonstrated a strong dedication to fostering learning and moral values. Amb. Ahmad Manir is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators (FICMC), a Senior Member of the Institute of Global Peace and Conflict Management (SIGPCM), and a Chartered Global Arbitrator (CGArb). Additionally, he is a respected member of the Nigerian Bar Association, where he actively contributes to the legal profession. As the founder of Zayyat Islamic Schools, Amb. Ahmad Manir has combined his expertise in education and law to establish an institution that nurtures academic excellence and Islamic ethics. His vision and leadership continue to inspire the school's commitment to developing morally upright and knowledgeable individuals who contribute positively to society.
